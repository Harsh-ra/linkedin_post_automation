# Instagram Caption Writer

For each asset (carousel / infographic / reel), write a caption to
`output/<DATE>/captions/<slot>.txt`.

## Structure (mandatory)
1. **Hook line** — the first 8–12 words must earn the tap on "... more".
   Mirror or extend the asset's on-slide hook; do not repeat it verbatim.
2. **Body** — 2–5 short lines, blank line between thoughts, one idea.
3. **CTA** — exactly one: Save / Share with a friend who / Follow @harshdecodeai / Comment "X".
4. **Hashtags** — 8–15 on their own block at the bottom. Mix:
   - Broad reach: #ai #artificialintelligence #futureofwork #productivity
   - Niche/topic: e.g. #aitools #careergrowth #aiautomation
   - Small brand tag: #harshdecodeai

## Rules
- No em-dashes. Specific numbers over adjectives. No banned words (see voice-profile.md).
- Caption ≤ 2200 chars (IG limit); aim for 60–125 words of body.
- First person is allowed lightly in captions (unlike slide copy).

## Example
```
AI won't replace you. The person who automates their boring 80% will.

Most people spend all day on tasks software could do in seconds.
The winners hand that off and pour the saved hours into the 20% only they can do.
That gap compounds every single week.

Save this so you remember which 20% to protect.

#ai #futureofwork #productivity #careergrowth #aitools #aiautomation #worksmarter #harshdecodeai
```
